Welcome to Website

Raghuraj Sharan Singh Mahavidyalaya is a leading educational institution of higher learning in Balpur, Dist.-Gonda. The college is located at Nakha Basant, Balpur. It is affiliated to Dr.Ram Manohar Lohia Avadh University, Faizabad.

Founded in 2012 by Mr.Brij Bhusan Singh Sharan Singh ji. This college at present runs Graduate and Postgraduate Courses in faculties of arts, science, commerce education and computer science with the dedication of teachers and non-teaching staff, the college is establishing newer hights in quality education.
