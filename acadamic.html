Admission Procedure

Admission in B.A., B.Sc., B.Com., M.A., M.Sc. offered by this college is strictly merit based. Reservation for SC / ST / OBC / HPPD / DFF are applicable as per up govt. rules. Admission in B.Ed. is through entrance examinations as per state govt. The application form with a brochure can be obtained from college office. Admission process begins in the month of April every year.
